Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 9, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how this personal TikTok analytics application handles data. This application is designed for individual use to analyze the owner's own TikTok account data.

2. Information We Access

This application accesses the following data from your TikTok account through the official TikTok API:

  • Account profile information (username, follower count)
  • Video analytics (views, likes, comments, shares)
  • Content performance metrics
  • Engagement statistics

3. How We Use Your Data

Your TikTok data is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying analytics and performance metrics
  • Creating visualizations and dashboards
  • Personal content strategy analysis

4. Data Storage

This application:

  • May temporarily store data locally in your browser for session purposes
  • Does not maintain long-term storage of your TikTok data
  • Does not store data on external servers beyond necessary API caching

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Access or collect data from other TikTok users

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure API communication using HTTPS
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Limited data retention

7. Third-Party Services

This application uses:

  • TikTok API: Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service
  • No other third-party analytics or tracking services

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke application access at any time through your TikTok account settings
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Access information about what data we process

9. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for the application to function. You can clear all data by:

  • Revoking application access in TikTok settings
  • Clearing your browser cache and local storage

10. Children's Privacy

This application is intended for users who are at least 18 years old or the age of majority in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. International Data Transfers

Data processing occurs through TikTok's API infrastructure and may be transferred across borders in accordance with TikTok's policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made.

13. Compliance

This application complies with:

  • TikTok Developer Terms of Service
  • TikTok API Developer Policy
  • Applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

14. Contact Information

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or data practices, you may contact through the TikTok developer portal or revoke access through your TikTok account settings.
